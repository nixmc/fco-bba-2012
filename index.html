Britons in trouble abroad

Total numbers of incidents requiring consular assistance from the Foreign and Commonwealth Office between April 2012 and March 2013, listed by country.

These figures have been compiled from records of consular assistance provided by the Foreign and Commonwealth Office between April 2012 and March 2013. The combined data is publicly available as a Google spreadsheet.
